About Us

Racing Edge Swim Club is a highly competitive swim team with a family-like atmoshphere. The goal of our team is to provide every swimmer an opportunity to improve swimming skills and achieve success at their own personal level of ability from novice to international competitor. Our coaches believe swimmers who are trained with personal attention and cutting edge technique, become athletes who are equipped to compete at the highest level. We are a yearly round swim club providing you with our own pool and gym. All you have to do is be here and stay commited!
